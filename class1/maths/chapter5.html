📚 Chapter 5: Comparing Numbers

Class 1 - Mathematics | Fun Learning

🎯 What is Comparing?

Comparing means finding out which number is bigger, smaller, or if they are equal!

We use special symbols to compare numbers:

🌟 Comparison Symbols

Greater Than

>

The crocodile eats the bigger number!

5 > 3

5 is greater than 3

Less Than

<

The crocodile eats the bigger number!

2 < 4

2 is less than 4

Equal To

=

Both numbers are the same!

3 = 3

3 is equal to 3

🐊 Crocodile Trick!

Remember: The crocodile always eats the bigger number!

7 🐊 3

The crocodile's mouth opens towards 7 because 7 is bigger!

7 > 3

1 🐊 5

The crocodile's mouth opens towards 5 because 5 is bigger!

1 < 5

🎈 More Examples

Example 1: Comparing with Pictures

3 apples 🍎🍎🍎 vs 5 apples 🍎🍎🍎🍎🍎

3 < 5

3 is less than 5

Example 2: Same Amount

4 stars ⭐⭐⭐⭐ vs 4 stars ⭐⭐⭐⭐

4 = 4

4 is equal to 4

💡 Fun Fact!

🌟 The > and < symbols always point to the smaller number!

🌟 Think of them as a hungry crocodile's mouth!

🌟 The wide side is near the bigger number!
