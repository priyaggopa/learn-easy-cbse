Chapter 1: Numbers 🔢

Let's learn about wonderful numbers!

What are Numbers? 🤔

Numbers are special symbols we use to count things! Just like we have letters to make words, we have numbers to count and measure.

Numbers help us answer questions like: How many toys do you have? How old are you? How many friends are playing with you?

Fun Fact! ⭐

Did you know? The number ZERO (0) is very special! It means "nothing" or "none". If you have zero cookies, it means you have no cookies! 🍪

⭐⭐ - How many stars? (2 stars!)

🎈🎈🎈 - How many balloons? (3 balloons!)

🌸🌸🌸🌸 - How many flowers? (4 flowers!)

Remember! 💡

Numbers are everywhere! You can find them on clocks, phones, books, and even on your birthday cake with candles! Keep practicing counting every day!
