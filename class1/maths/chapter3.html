🎯 What is Subtraction?

Subtraction means taking away! When we subtract, we find out how many things are left.

5 - 2 = 3

We use the - (minus) sign to subtract numbers!

🌟 Let's Learn with Pictures!

Example 1: Taking Away Balloons 🎈

You have 5 balloons:

🎈🎈🎈🎈🎈

2 balloons fly away:

🎈🎈

Now you have:

🎈🎈🎈
5 - 2 = 3

Example 2: Eating Cookies 🍪

You have 4 cookies:

🍪🍪🍪🍪

You eat 1 cookie:

🍪

Cookies left:

🍪🍪🍪
4 - 1 = 3

💡 Fun Fact!

When we subtract zero (0) from any number, the number stays the same!

5 - 0 = 5 ✨

Also, when we subtract a number from itself, we get zero!

5 - 5 = 0 🎉
