Chapter 3: Subtraction ➖

Let's learn to take away and subtract!

What is Subtraction? 🤔

Subtraction means taking things away! When we subtract, we remove some things and count how many are left.

We use the minus sign (-) to show subtraction. The answer we get is called the DIFFERENCE!

Fun Fact! 💡

When we subtract zero (0) from any number, the number stays the same!

5 - 0 = 5 ✨

Also, when we subtract a number from itself, we always get zero!

5 - 5 = 0 🎉
