Chapter 3: Introduction to Multiplication ✖️

Multiplication means adding the same number again and again!

Example:
🍩🍩🍩 + 🍩🍩🍩 = 6 donuts (That’s 3 + 3)
We can also write this as: 3 × 2 = 6

Fun Fact! ⭐

Multiplication is a quick way to add equal groups together.

Practice Time! ✏️

  • How much is 2 × 4?
  • Draw two rows of three apples each. How many apples in total?
  • What is 5 × 1?

Remember! 💡

Any number times 1 stays the same!
