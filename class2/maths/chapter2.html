Chapter 2: Addition and Subtraction ➕➖

Let’s learn how to add (join) and subtract (take away) numbers!

Addition Example:
🍎🍎 + 🍎 = 3 apples
Subtraction Example:
🦉🦉🦉 - 🦉 = 2 owls left

Fun Fact! ⭐

In subtraction, the answer is called a “difference.” In addition, the answer is a “sum.”

Practice Time! ✏️

  • What is 6 + 3?
  • What is 10 - 4?
  • Draw 5 flowers, then cross out 2. How many are left?

Remember! 💡

Addition makes numbers bigger, subtraction makes them smaller!
